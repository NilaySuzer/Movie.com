Me Before You is a 2016 romantic drama film based on the novel by Jojo Moyes. It tells the story of Louisa Clark, a cheerful and quirky young woman who becomes the caregiver for Will Traynor, a wealthy and once-adventurous man left paralyzed after an accident. As Louisa helps Will rediscover joy in life, they develop a deep and complicated bond that challenges their perspectives on love, independence, and difficult choices. The film explores themes of compassion, personal freedom, and the impact of relationships.
