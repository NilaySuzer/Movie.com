Titanic (1997) is a romantic drama directed by James Cameron. It tells the story of Jack and Rose, two passengers from very different social classes who meet and fall in love aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic during its maiden voyage. As their romance blossoms, the ship tragically hits an iceberg and begins to sink, forcing them to fight for survival. The film blends epic disaster with heartfelt love, exploring themes of class divide, sacrifice, and enduring love.
