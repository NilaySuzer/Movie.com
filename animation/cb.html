IMDb: 7.4/10

Corpse Bride is a 2005 stop-motion animated film directed by Tim Burton. It tells the story of Victor, a nervous young man who accidentally proposes to a mysterious deceased woman named Emily while practicing his wedding vows in the forest. Taken to the Land of the Dead, Victor must navigate between two worlds to figure out where his heart truly belongs. The film blends dark humor, romance, and gothic visuals, exploring themes of love, commitment, and letting go.
